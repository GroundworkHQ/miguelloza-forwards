What we do

Services built by hand, made to endure

From the first sketch to the final stone, we handle every part of your outdoor space so it comes together as one cohesive, lasting design.

01 — Landscape Design

Landscape Design

We design custom landscapes around native plants suited to our Northeast Georgia climate, so your yard thrives with less fuss. Whether you want a low-maintenance retreat or a hands-on gardener's paradise, we shape the space around how you actually want to live outdoors.

  • Native, climate-appropriate plantings
  • Spaces for entertaining, relaxing, and connecting with nature
  • Designs balanced for beauty and everyday function
02 — Hardscape

Hardscapes

Patios, walkways, retaining walls, and fire pits that give your landscape structure and turn it into an outdoor living room. We build in natural stone and pavers, engineered to hold up to the seasons and look better with every year.

  • Patios, walkways, and steps
  • Retaining walls and boulder walls
  • Fire pits and outdoor living features
03 — Seasonal Installations

Seasonal Installations

Our quarterly installation service keeps your landscape vibrant all year long. We handle seasonal plantings and timely refreshes so your garden evolves gracefully with each change of season, and always looks its best.

  • Quarterly seasonal plantings
  • Refreshes timed to the growing calendar
  • Year-round color and curb appeal
04 — Rock Features

Rock Features

Custom stone ponds, cascading waterfalls, dry creek beds, and decorative rock arrangements, each crafted to match your style. These are the details that turn a landscape into a peaceful sanctuary you never want to leave.

  • Stone ponds and cascading waterfalls
  • Dry creek beds and drainage features
  • Decorative boulder and rock arrangements
