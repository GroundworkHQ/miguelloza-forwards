What we do

Premium Landscape Construction, Hardscapes & Outdoor Living Services

Manscaped Outdoors designs and builds complete outdoor spaces for mountain and lake properties across Northeast Georgia and nearby Western North Carolina. Our work combines landscape design, patios, retaining walls, boulder walls, drainage, grading, decks, pergolas, stonework, planting, and finish details into one cohesive outdoor environment.

Landscape Design Patios & Hardscapes Retaining Walls Drainage & Grading Decks & Pergolas Full Transformations
01 · Landscape Design & Installation

Landscape Design & Installation

A strong landscape starts with a plan. We design planting layouts, bed lines, transitions, focal points, access paths, and outdoor spaces around your home, your property, and how you want to live outside. Our planting work is built around mountain conditions: sun, shade, slope, clay, deer pressure, drainage, and long-term maintenance.

  • Landscape design, layout, and planting design
  • Bed shaping, focal points, and access paths
  • Mountain home and lake house landscaping
  • Low-maintenance, climate-appropriate installation
02 · Patios, Hardscapes & Outdoor Living

Patios, Hardscapes & Outdoor Living

Hardscapes give structure to the landscape. We build patios, walkways, fire pit areas, seating spaces, paver areas, flagstone patios, natural stone features, and outdoor gathering spaces that turn unused yards into places worth spending time.

  • Paver, stone, and flagstone patios
  • Walkways, steps, and seating areas
  • Fire pit patios and outdoor fireplaces
  • Outdoor living and entertaining spaces
03 · Retaining Walls & Boulder Walls

Retaining Walls & Boulder Walls

In the mountains, retaining walls are not just decorative. They manage grade, control erosion, create usable space, and protect the property when built correctly. We build boulder walls, natural stone walls, manufactured block walls, stone steps, and failed wall rebuilds with attention to drainage, base preparation, backfill, and long-term performance.

  • Boulder walls and natural stone retaining walls
  • Segmental and block retaining walls
  • Stone steps and slope stabilization
  • Retaining wall repair and failed wall rebuilds
04 · Drainage, Grading & Erosion Control

Drainage, Grading & Erosion Control

Water decides whether an outdoor project lasts. Poor drainage can ruin patios, walls, turf, plantings, driveways, and foundations. We solve runoff, washouts, standing water, slope erosion, poor grading, and failed drainage patterns with practical systems built for North Georgia terrain.

  • French drains, dry creek beds, and downspout drainage
  • Yard drainage, driveway drainage, and runoff control
  • Grading, yard grading, and erosion control
  • Drainage behind retaining walls and slope erosion repair
05 · Decks, Pergolas & Outdoor Structures

Decks, Pergolas & Outdoor Structures

Some outdoor spaces need more than stone and planting. Manscaped Outdoors also builds decks, pergolas, privacy screens, stairs, railings, wood accents, and outdoor structures that extend the living space and tie the built environment into the landscape.

  • Deck construction, replacement, and railings
  • Composite and wood decks with stairs
  • Pergolas, covered patios, and outdoor pavilions
  • Privacy screens and outdoor structures
06 · Full Property Transformations

Full Property Transformations

Some properties need more than one service. They need a coordinated plan. For larger projects, we bring together walls, drainage, grading, patios, decks, planting, turf, access, and finish details so the property feels complete, not pieced together over time.

  • Backyard and complete landscape renovations
  • Outdoor living design across multiple services
  • High-end, estate, mountain home, and lake house landscaping
  • One cohesive plan from grading to finish details
