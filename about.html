Meet Jared

Grown from Georgia soil

Manscaped Outdoors was founded by Jared, who grew up right here in Northeast Georgia. Some of his earliest memories are of working in the yard and garden alongside his grandparents and parents, learning the value of hard work and a deep appreciation for the outdoors.

After college, landscaping wasn't something he left behind for another path. It was the calling he came back to. Today Jared is still on site with his crew, hands in the dirt, turning each client's vision into something real.

For us, the work goes beyond landscaping. It's about building relationships, creating lasting beauty, and sharing the peace that comes from a well-tended garden, an outdoor space where you truly feel at home.

We don't just build landscapes. We build relationships, lasting beauty, and the quiet peace of a place that finally feels like home.
