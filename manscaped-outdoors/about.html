Our story

Rooted in Northeast Georgia. Built for serious outdoor work.

Manscaped Outdoors was founded to do outdoor work the right way, with craftsmanship, clear communication, and respect for the land.

Meet Jared

From a local landscape company to a premium design-build

Manscaped Outdoors was founded by Jared, who grew up right here in Northeast Georgia. Some of his earliest memories are of working in the yard alongside his grandparents and parents, learning the value of hard work and a deep appreciation for the outdoors.

What started as a local landscape company has grown into a premium outdoor design-build company serving mountain and lake properties with hardscapes, retaining walls, drainage, grading, stonework, decks, planting, and complete outdoor transformations. Today Jared is still on site with his crew, hands in the dirt, holding every project to the same standard.

For us, the work goes beyond landscaping. It is about understanding the property, solving the real problems, and building outdoor spaces that hold up and keep earning their place for years to come.

Our approach

Beauty matters. Structure comes first.

A beautiful patio does not matter if it drains poorly. A retaining wall does not matter if the base fails. A planting plan does not matter if the grading sends water through the beds. Our approach starts with the practical realities of the site: slope, drainage, access, soil, structure, material choice, and long-term use.
